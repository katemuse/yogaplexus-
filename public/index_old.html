Welcome

Yoga Plexus is an online service for yoga teachers. It helps you plan class, create sequences and refresh your memory of sanskrit and various yoga techniques. You can submit comments on the asanas (postures) share your teaching experiences, and read other teacher's perspectives. Once you register and log in you will be directed to a homepage on Yoga Plexus that will show show your sequences and your submissions to various asana pages. You will have access to an “asana sequence panel” that lists asanas by type and lets you click one at a time to create your own sequence, which then gets saved to your homepage. You can open each sequence in concise or “exploded” view to get the main information on each posture in a printable form.
